10 Things You Need to Know About the 2022 Infiniti QX60

Tesla’s stamp on the electric car is impossible to overstate. It wasn’t the first company to sell a lot of fully electric vehicles, but its lineup of sleek, fast luxury cars proved that EVs’ appeal could extend beyond everyday eco-minded fuel-savers. While other EVs were cheap cars with expensive batteries that needed frequent recharges, early Teslas were unapologetically expensive — but delivered the goods. They were functional, fast, and downright cool.
