9. 2021 Kia Seltos

One big advantage of the Kia Seltos is the fact that for a high-fuel-economy subcompact SUV, it is big. Its interior offers very accommodating seating for five, and its cargo hold is also commodious. The 26.6 cubic feet of cargo space behind the rear seats is among the biggest in the class, as is the 62.8 cubic feet of space with the rear seat folded. With the most thrifty four-cylinder engine, power is not its strong suit, however.
